Decision-Making Wheel Activity

Exploring the Problem-Solving Framework

The Scenario

Two faculty members in the same department are not getting along. Their conflict is beginning to affect department meetings and collaborative projects.

How This Activity Works

  1. Click the "Spin the Wheel" button below
  2. The wheel will land on one section of the decision-making framework
  3. Consider the prompt that appears: How might you apply this particular approach to the faculty conflict?
  4. Take 1-2 minutes to brainstorm or discuss your ideas
  5. Spin again to explore a different perspective (try 3-4 sections total)
